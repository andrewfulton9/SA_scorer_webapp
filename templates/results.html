Column Descriptions

group: animals group
weight_percentage: percent of max weight at time of testing
alternations: total # of alternations
arm entries: total arm entries
% alternation: alternations divided by arm entries - 3
% perseverative errors: number of times a subject entered an arm, entered another arm, and then returned to previous arm (ex: 1,2,1) divided by arm entries - 2
% repeat entries: number of times a subject entered an arm immediatly after leaving that same arm without entering another arm in between divided by arm entries - 1
% 1 entries: number of times subject entered arm 1 divided by arm entries
% 2 entries: number of times subject entered arm 2 divided by arm entries
% 3 entries: number of times subject entered arm 3 divided by arm entries
% 4 entries: number of times subject entered arm 4 divided by arm entries
arm entry inequality: the average percent difference from entering each arm 25% of the time.
