Welcome to SA Scorer!

SA Scorer is a web application built for the purpose of scoring raw data collected from spontaneous alternation using a 4-armed plus maze. The goal of this app is to streamline and eliminate human error in scoring.

Begin by downloading the excel template for data entry. Once you have filled in your raw data, click on the upload file link here or above in the header. Next upload your completed file and choose whether you would like to score the data from the full 18 minutes, the first 6 minutes or the first 12 minutes and press submit and a table of scored data will be returned to you, which you can then download {% endblock %} Home
